        Rice Wireless welcomes prospective PhD students, postdocs, and
        visiting researchers interested in wireless networks,
        information theory, signal processing, and machine learning for
        next-generation communications.

        ### PhD Applications
        Apply through the Rice ECE or CS graduate admissions portal.
        In your application, name the Rice Wireless faculty whose work
        most aligns with your interests.

        ### Postdoctoral Positions
        Postdoc openings are advertised on individual faculty pages and
        through the Rice ECE/CS career sites. Reach out directly to
        faculty whose work matches your background.

        ### Visiting Researchers
        We host short- and long-term visiting researchers, including
        sabbatical visitors and visiting PhD students. Contact
        [wireless@rice.edu](mailto:wireless@rice.edu).
