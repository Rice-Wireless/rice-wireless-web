The paper *"Uplink Multi-User Beamforming on Single RF Chain mmWave
WLANs,"* authored by **Keerthi Priya Dasala**, **Edward Knightly**, and
Josep M. Jornet (Northeastern University), won the Best Paper Award at
**IEEE INFOCOM 2021**.
